Let's get started!

NCT Dream, the third sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, was formed by SM Entertainment in 2016. They were initially intended to be the teenaged unit of NCT with an admission-and-graduation system, in which members would leave after reaching age of majority, prior to re-branding in 2020.

Mark Lee

Mark is the leader and main rapper of NCT DREAM. Born on August 2, 1999, in Canada, he is fluent in both English and Korean. Known for his strong rap skills and songwriting, Mark is also active in other NCT units.

Huang Renjun

Renjun is a main vocalist in NCT DREAM, born on March 23, 2000, in China. He is known for his clear and emotional voice, as well as his artistic personality. Renjun is fluent in both Mandarin and Korean.

Lee Jeno

Jeno is a rapper and dancer, born on April 23, 2000, in South Korea. Known for his sharp movements and calm personality, he started as a child model and grew into a confident performer. He enjoys photography, working out, and quietly supporting his members behind the scenes.

Lee Donghyuck (Haechan)

Haechan is the main vocalist of NCT DREAM, born on June 6, 2000, in South Korea. He is known for his warm, emotional vocal tone that adds depth to every song. A member of NCT 127 and NCT U as well, Haechan stands out for his playful personality, quick wit, and bright presence both on and off the stage.

Na Jaemin

Jaemin is a multi-talented performer born on August 13, 2000, in Jeonju, South Korea. He serves as a rapper, vocalist, and dancer in NCT DREAM. Widely admired for his visuals and stage charisma, Jaemin also has a gentle, caring personality. Off-stage, heâ€™s known for his volunteer work and thoughtful messages to fans.

Zhong Chenle

Chenle is a main vocalist of NCT DREAM, born on November 22, 2001, in Shanghai, China. He gained recognition as a child star before joining NCT. Known for his powerful high notes and joyful laugh, Chenle brings a lively energy to the group. He is also passionate about basketball and often plays piano during free time.

Park Jisung

Jisung is the youngest member (maknae) of NCT DREAM, born on February 5, 2002, in Seoul, South Korea. Renowned for his smooth and precise dancing, Jisung began training at a very young age. Despite being the maknae, he shows a mature and calm presence, with a deep voice and a love for gaming and songwriting.

NCT DREAM debuted in 2016 as the "youth unit" of NCT. Their music evolved from bubbly teen pop to mature, experimental sounds while keeping their original energy.

NCT DREAM has received numerous awards, including the "Best New Artist" at the 2017 Mnet Asian Music Awards. Their impact on the K-pop scene is significant, with a dedicated fanbase and critical acclaim.
